IdeaClaw 是一套面向学术研究与高规格商业场景的结构化生成引擎。结合大模型推演与自愈编排管线，彻底消除长文本推理中的结构崩塌与逻辑遗漏问题。 IdeaClaw is a Structured Autonomous Generation Engine engineered for academic and high-stakes industry scenarios. Combining LLM inference with a self-healing orchestration pipeline, it eliminates structural collapse and logical omissions in long-form generation.

底层核心架构 System Architecture

旨在通过主动自愈反馈回路与严格的执行契约，大幅降低大型语言模型（LLMs）在格式输出上的灾难性失效。 Engineered to mitigate catastrophic formatting failures in Large Language Models (LLMs) through active self-healing feedback loops and strict execution contracts.

模板驱动执行 Template Execution

内置 131 套严密的场景结构体。系统基于 MECE 框架拉出高级骨架大纲，提供超越单一提示词的确定性输出。 Loaded with 131 rigorous structural templates. The system derives a top-tier outline based on the MECE framework, providing deterministic output far beyond traditional prompts.

Salacia 控制护栏 Salacia Guardrails

弃用黑盒形式的提示词工程，改用具体的执行契约（Execution Contract），将计算引擎置于严格的结构化输入输出约束中。 Bypassing black-box prompt engineering in favor of concrete Execution Contracts, placing the computational engine under strict structured I/O constraints.

结构域自愈 Structural Self-Healing

针对超长文本的 Token 熔断，构建自动化截断恢复管线。若发现 LLM 截断或漏写，系统将在后台自动捕获错误追踪并重试。 Actively mitigates Token truncation during hyper-long generations. If structural omissions are detected, the system catches error traces and autonomously retries until perfection.
